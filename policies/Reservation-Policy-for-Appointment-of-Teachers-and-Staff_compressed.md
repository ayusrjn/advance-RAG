## Reservation Policy for Teaching &amp; Non-teaching Staff

Forrecruitment andpromotionsof Teaching andNon-teachingStaff,theUniversityfollows thereservationpolicy enacted by the Government of India as amendedfrom time to time.

The University of Hyderabad has adopted the policy of reservations in respect of SCs,STs, OBCs,PWD and EWSwherever applicable,asper the Government of India/UGC orders. Reservations are being provided to the extent of 15%,7.5%, 27%, 4% and 10% respectively in direct recruitment,promotions (excl. OBC &amp; EWS categories)and 5% for PWD in admissions and hostel accommodation besides allotment of quarters(excl. OBC&amp; EWs categories)to teaching and non-teaching employees.

In terms of fulfilmentof SC/ST/OBC/PWD/EWS quotasin admissions,theUniversity is able to achieve an overall representation of SC-16.44%,ST-7.93%;OBC-36.3%,PwD2.7%,EWS-5.47%in student admissions during the year 2019-20.
