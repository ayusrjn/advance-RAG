## Policy of University of Hyderabad Students Assistance (UoH-SA)

## 1. Inviting Applications:

- a) The students whose parental income is less than 8 lakhs and who fulfill the criteria are provided financial assistance @ Rs. 1000/- per month to the maximum of 1200 students. 600 students admitted in Masters and Integrated Master Programme for a period of 10 months in an academic year. And 600 to ongoing students of Masters and IntegratedMasterProgramme.
